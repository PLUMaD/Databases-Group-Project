How to Play

Rule #1

A question will be shown with an inserted word from the database.

Rule #2

Below, two buttons will also hold choices given by the database.

Rule #3

After clicking on one, you will be given the proportion of people who choose the different options.



What is this game?

This is an HTML endpoint that directs the user to various pages according to their choice of two options.

Options are designed like a tree, expanding upon the previous to produce more detailed data on a given topic and keyword.

Choices are saved to our database and are displayed on a results page following the user's selection.
